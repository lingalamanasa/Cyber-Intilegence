Our Mission

We Believe Every Organization Deserves Protection

Founded in 2019, Stackly was born from a simple conviction: cybersecurity shouldn't be a luxury reserved for Fortune 500 companies. Every business, hospital, school, and government agency deserves the same level of protection against digital threats.

Our team of world-class security researchers, threat analysts, and engineers works tirelessly to develop cutting-edge solutions that are powerful yet accessible. We combine artificial intelligence with human expertise to deliver unmatched threat intelligence and incident response capabilities.
